## 1.maven设置版本号

mvn versions:set -DnewVersion=0.0.2-SNAPSHOT
<!--more-->

## 2.maven release忽略snapshots

mvn release:prepare -DignoreSnapshots=true -Darguments="-DskipTests"

mvn release:perform -DuseReleaseProfile=false -Darguments="-DskipTests"
